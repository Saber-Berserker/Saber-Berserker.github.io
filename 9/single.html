吕布（天魔缭乱）

Lv Bu was initially positioned as a fighter. After several versions of the update, Lv Bu was positioned as a fighter and a tank. Lv Bu was able to fight, slow down, control, recover, and harvest. With the flash, it had more unexpected effects. In the 5v5 classic battlefield, it was the presence of the enemy's fear（吕布最初被定位为战士，在几次版本更新后，吕布的定位是战士和坦克双重定位，吕布能打能抗，有减速有控制有回复，而且还能收割，配合闪现更有令人想不到的效果，在5V5经典战场中是令敌人闻风丧胆的存在）
